Gestalt Color Studies

Design Fundamentals I

Fall 2017


This is a project from my first Graphic Design class at GW. Gestalt is a psychological theory that has created laws of human perception. These principals are essential to the foundation of Graphic Design, so this project helped us understand how objects are perceived and interact. After arranging objects within a square, we took the best design and used it as a foundation to explore the basic concepts of color theory. The images shown here are from the color theory portion of this course.
